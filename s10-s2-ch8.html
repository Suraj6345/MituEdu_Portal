Cell Biology and Biotechnology

Q1.

What are Stem Cells and their uses?

  • Stem cells are undifferentiated cells that have the capacity to form any type of cell in the body.
  • Regenerative Therapy: Used to replace damaged cells in conditions like Diabetes or Myocardial Infarction.
  • Organ Transplantation: Producing organs like kidneys and livers in laboratories from stem cells.
Q2.

Explain the benefits of Biotechnology in Agriculture.

  • Hybrid Seeds: Increase in yield and quality of crops.
  • GM Crops (Genetically Modified): Pest-resistant varieties like Bt Cotton and Golden Rice (rich in Vitamin A).
  • Better resistance to environmental stress like cold and drought.
