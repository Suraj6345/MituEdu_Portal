Heredity and Evolution

Q1.

Explain the process of Transcription.

  • Transcription is the process of synthesis of mRNA as per the sequence of nucleotides on DNA.
  • Only one of the two strands of DNA is used in this process.
  • The sequence of nucleotides in mRNA being produced is always complementary to the DNA strand used for synthesis.
  • This process occurs inside the nucleus.
Q2.

Define Evolution and mention its evidences.

Evolution: It is the gradual change occurring in living organisms over a long duration.

  • Morphological Evidences: Similarities in external features like shape of mouth or position of eyes.
  • Anatomical Evidences: Similarities in bone structure (e.g., human hand and foreleg of ox).
  • Vestigial Organs: Degenerated or underdeveloped useless organs (e.g., Appendix, Wisdom teeth).
