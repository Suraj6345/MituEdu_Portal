Life Processes in Living Organisms - Part 2

Q1.

Explain Double Fertilization in Plants.

  • Pollen grains reach the stigma via pollination and germinate.
  • Two male gametes are released through the pollen tube.
  • One male gamete unites with the egg cell to form a zygote.
  • The second male gamete unites with two polar nuclei to form the endosperm.
Q2.

What are the methods of Asexual Reproduction in unicellular organisms?

  • Binary Fission: The parent cell divides into two equal daughter cells (e.g., Amoeba).
  • Multiple Fission: Occurs during unfavorable conditions (e.g., Amoeba forming a cyst).
  • Budding: A small bud comes out from the parent cell (e.g., Yeast).
