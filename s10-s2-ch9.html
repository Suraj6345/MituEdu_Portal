Social Health

Q1.

What factors affect Social Health?

  • Availability of food, water, and clothing.
  • Education and financial status.
  • Social and political stability.
  • Addiction and Cybercrime: These are modern threats to social health.
Q2.

Explain the importance of stress management.

  • Stress leads to mental illness and physical diseases like high blood pressure.
  • Methods include: Laughter clubs, hobbies (singing, reading), regular exercise, and communication with friends/family.
