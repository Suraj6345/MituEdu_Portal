Life Processes in Living Organisms - Part 1

Q1.

Explain the process of Glycolysis.

  • It is the first step of aerobic and anaerobic respiration.
  • It occurs in the cytoplasm of the cell.
  • One molecule of glucose is oxidized step-by-step into two molecules each of: Pyruvic acid, ATP, NADH2, and Water.
Q2.

Distinguish between Mitosis and Meiosis.

  • Mitosis: Occurs in somatic cells. Produces 2 daughter cells. Number of chromosomes remains the same ($2n \rightarrow 2n$).
  • Meiosis: Occurs in germ cells. Produces 4 daughter cells. Number of chromosomes is halved ($2n \rightarrow n$).
