Environmental Management

Q1.

Explain the three levels of Biodiversity.

  • Genetic Diversity: Diversity among organisms of the same species (e.g., each human is different).
  • Species Diversity: Innumerable species of organisms occur in nature (e.g., plants, animals, microbes).
  • Ecosystem Diversity: Many ecosystems (forests, deserts, ponds) exist in a region.
Q2.

What is your role in Environmental conservation?

  • Prevention: Preventing possible hazards and stopping harmful activities.
  • Control: Controlling the current pollution and changing old mindsets.
  • Production: Planting more trees and protecting local species.
  • Awareness: Education, guidance, and organization for protection.
