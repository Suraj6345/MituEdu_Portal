Towards Green Energy

Q1.

Why is Thermal energy not considered eco-friendly?

  • Burning of coal releases harmful gases like CO2, SO2, and NO2.
  • It leads to Air Pollution and global warming.
  • Coal is a non-renewable (limited) source of energy.
  • It causes health respiratory problems in humans.
Q2.

What are the advantages of Hydroelectric Energy?

  • No fuel is burnt, so no air pollution.
  • Water can be reused as it is a renewable source.
  • Continuous supply of electricity is possible as long as there is water in the dam.
