Introduction to Microbiology

Q1.

Explain the importance of Probiotics.

  • Probiotics are milk products containing active bacteria (e.g., Lactobacillus).
  • They maintain the balance of intestinal microorganisms by increasing "good" bacteria.
  • They improve immunity and decrease the harmful effects of pathogens.
Q2.

What are the uses of microbial enzymes?

  • They are used in the cheese, textile, and paper industries.
  • Unlike chemical catalysts, they work at low temperatures and pH, saving energy.
  • They are eco-friendly and reduce the cost of waste disposal.
